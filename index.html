Exam Interview Answers

5. What does it mean to have good programming practices?

Good programming practices are essential to being a good collaborator and team person, they help the workflow of the team run smoothly and with harmony. The main programming practice that most of the smaller ones fall under, would be to write readable code, even someone who isn’t that experienced in computer science should be able to read and understand your code. Some more specific practices related to readable code are: documenting your code so it’s easier for someone to understand what specific lines are doing, being consistent with indentation and naming schemes, grouping code, etc. Having good programming practices can also help a lot with getting a computer programming job, and help you keep it, by letting yourself appear as a very good team player.

Having good programming practices also involves the planning stages in designing a software or website. Writing pseudocode, and even before that, having a concrete idea of what you want the final result to be. Pseudocode should allow you to follow a plan and to complete the basis of the software or website before getting lost in the extra and not so necessary details and functions. Good programming practices not just helps the people working with you, but also improves your own workflow significantly.

10. How can art help guide you through learning about programming and computer science?

Without art and creativity, what would the web be? A place with only plain and boring websites with facts and news, but nothing with games or interesting visuals. Art is one of the most important things when it comes to thinking up ideas for almost all types of programs. When you have an idea for a software or a website that you want to become a reality, you may need to research things that you haven’t learnt yet. It could be a specific piece of code or a whole area of computer science that you didn’t even know about before. Having these ideas and being creative, forces you to learn new things and expand your horizons of what you are capable of, that in the long term, can really benefit your career and happiness.
